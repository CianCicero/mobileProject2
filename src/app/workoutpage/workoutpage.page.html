WEIGHTS WEIGHTS Squat A compound exercise where you lower your body into a squat position and then push through your heels to lift the weight, primarily targeting your lower body muscles. Deadlift This exercise involves lifting a loaded barbell from the ground to an upright position, working multiple muscle groups including your back, hips, and legs. Bench Press By lying flat on a bench and pressing a weighted barbell away from your chest, the bench press primarily targets your chest, shoulders, and triceps. Overhead Press Also known as the shoulder press, it involves pressing a barbell or dumbbells overhead from shoulder level, targeting your shoulders, triceps, and upper back. Pull-up Using an overhead bar, this exercise involves pulling your body up until your chin passes the bar, engaging your back, shoulders, and arms for a challenging upper body workout.
