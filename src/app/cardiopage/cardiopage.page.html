CARDIO CARDIO Running Running involves moving rapidly on foot, typically at a steady pace, and is a highly effective cardiovascular exercise that engages multiple muscle groups and improves endurance. Jumping Rope Jumping rope involves continuously jumping over a rope swung under the feet, which helps elevate heart rate, improve coordination, and strengthen the lower body. Cycling Cycling, whether on a stationary bike or outdoors, is a low-impact exercise that strengthens the legs, boosts cardiovascular fitness, and can be done at varying intensities to suit different fitness levels. High-Intensity Interval Training HIIT workouts involve alternating between short bursts of intense exercise (like burpees or jumping jacks) and brief recovery periods, providing a challenging cardio workout that maximizes calorie burn in less time.
